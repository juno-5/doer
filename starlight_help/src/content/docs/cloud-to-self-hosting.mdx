You can move freely between [Doer Cloud](https://zulip.com/doer-cloud/)
hosting and [self-hosting](https://zulip.com/self-hosting/): you own
your data.

## Move from Doer Cloud to self-hosting

<Steps>
  1. [Export](/help/export-your-organization) your organization's data from
     Doer Cloud.
  1. Follow [the instructions][import-only] to import your data into a
     self-hosted server.
</Steps>
