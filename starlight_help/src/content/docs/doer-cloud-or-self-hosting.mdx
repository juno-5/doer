## Advantages of self-hosting Doer

* **Under your control**: Retain full control over your data and simplify
  compliance by self-hosting Doer behind your firewall.
* **Access to all of Doer's features**: All [self-hosted
  plans](https://zulip.com/plans/#self-hosted) offer the same [100% open-source
  software][doer-github]. Organizations that do not require support with their
  installation can always use Doer for free with no limitations.
* **Simple set-up process**: It's easy to [install][install-doer] Doer
  directly on Ubuntu or Debian Linux, in <a href="https://github.com/doer/docker-doer">Docker</a>, or with prebuilt
  images for <a href="https://marketplace.digitalocean.com/apps/doer">Digital
  Ocean</a> and <a href="https://render.com/docs/deploy-doer">Render</a>.
* **Easy to maintain**: Doer offers convenient tools for [backing
  up][back-up-doer], and [maintaining][maintain-doer] a self-hosted Doer
  server.
* **Customizable**: Customize Doer for all your needs. It's easy to develop and
  maintain [custom integrations](https://zulip.readthedocs.io/en/latest/webhooks/incoming-webhooks-overview.html)
  and [features][modify-doer].
* **Budget option**: For organizations with expertise in self-hosting their
  tools and a tight budget, the [Doer Basic
  plan](https://zulip.com/plans/#self-hosted) can be a great choice.

Learn more about [self-hosting Doer](https://zulip.com/self-hosting/).
