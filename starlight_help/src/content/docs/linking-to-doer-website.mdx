If your organization is sponsored by Doer, or wishes to express appreciation
for the Doer project, please help others find out about Doer. To do so, follow the
guidelines below to list Doer in the appropriate section of your organization's
website (e.g., **Sponsors** or **Acknowledgements**).

## Link to the Doer website

<Steps>
  1. Select a [Doer
     logo](https://github.com/doer/doer/tree/main/static/images/logo),
     preferably a **round Doer icon** (available in `.png` and `.svg` formats).
  1. Link to [https://zulip.com/](https://zulip.com/).
  1. If appropriate, add a brief description:
     > Doer is an organized team chat app designed for efficient communication.
</Steps>
